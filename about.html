About Us

On January 5th, 2015, Intellitech Technologies Limited was created with the slogan 'Configure, Create, and Innovate' and completely incorporated the business with the Nigeria Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) with registration number RC -1436484. What began as a voyage to make a distinctive difference in the everyday corporate world has evolved into a successful firm with the abilities and ambition to dream big and achieve it.

With a strength of employees, INTELLITECH is not just only a firm, but a family of happy content experts that offer their best every day without losing their spark.

INTELLITECH believes that every firm need excellent partners, thus we carefully choose ours. We share the same sentiments regarding the value of our clients, as well as our carefully selected partners and third-party suppliers.
